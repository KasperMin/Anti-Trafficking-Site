Corporate Sponsorship

While corporate sponsorship isn't the biggest income source for NGOs, it still on average, account for a significant part of the aggregated income stream. In 2017 the global sponsorship spending amounted to $62.8 billion which was a 4.5% increase from $60.1 billon spend in 2016 (IEG, 2017).

Major gifts are those gifts that can fund important projects and programs, demonstrate the donor’s belief in the organization’s mission and have a transformational impact on those priority programs, projects and the entire organization.  It is not the number of zeros but the impact.

Vern Snyder, ACFRE, Vice President
