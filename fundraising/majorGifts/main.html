Major Gifts

When it comes to different types of revenue streams, your NGO can stay afloat by relying on grants, events, and small donations. But the only way to become a great NGO is by venturing into the business of Major Gifts. The reason why, is that major gift fundraising is far more efficient – it takes less time and is less work intensive – than either special events or grant writing.

What are Major Gifts?

Major gifts are basically big donations. But the aspect that differentiate major gifts from small donations, besides overtly the size, are the process by which they are obtained.
